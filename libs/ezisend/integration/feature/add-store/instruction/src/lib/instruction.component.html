How to connect

Step 1

Install the POS Malaysia Shopify plugin at Shopify store
(URL: https://apps.shopify.com/pos-malaysia-plugin)

Install Pos Malaysia in your WooCommerce store.
You may download Pos Malaysia plugin from the link below.

Pos Malaysia Plugin

downloadInstall Now

Step 2

Click Connect Now to be directed to WooCommerce page.

Login to your WooCommerce Store as Admin and navigate to Plugins > Add New and then click Upload Plugin.

Now, click "Choose File" to select Pos Malaysia plugin from your computer and then click Install Now.

Step 3

You will be directed to SendParcel Pro login page to activate Pos Malaysia plugin.

By simply enter your SendParcel Pro login details, your account will be authenticated, and your Pos Malaysia will be activated after successful login to SendParcel Pro.

Awesome! Now you can see Pos Malaysia plugin installed and activated in your WooCommerce store.
